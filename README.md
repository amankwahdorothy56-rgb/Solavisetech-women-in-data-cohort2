# Solavisetech-women-in-data-cohort2
# Program Introduction
This repository serves as my official workspace for the Solavisetech Women in data (Cohort2) training program. This program is designed specifically for women with high-demand data skills and this project particularly demonstrates my proficiency in version control and repository management using GitHub.
# About Me
I am Dorothy Amankwah, a second year Petrochemical Engineering student in the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi, Ghana. My academic career is driven by my passion for Organic Chemistry and Mathematics applied in solving societal problems. My technical background includes a virtual internship with petroskill and a field trip to Juabeng oil mills and Pokupharma company limited. I am proficient in a variety of technical tools including Python, Power BI and Excel and I hold a professional certification from Learniverse in the fundamentals of industrial office technology and also the fundamentals of the responsible use of Articial Intelligence.
